The studio

Afterthought is an independent design and creative studio in New York and Lisbon. We work with founders, institutions, and the occasional well-meaning monopoly on brand identity, naming, packaging, and digital product.

We take on roughly twelve engagements a year. New work for Autumn 2026 is open until the end of summer.
